Privacy Policy for Habit Seedling

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Habit Seedling is a local-first habit tracking app for Android. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Data Stored by the App

Habit Seedling stores the information you create inside the app on your device. This may include:

  • Habits, categories, icons, emojis, and tags
  • Habit progress, counters, timers, streaks, achievements, quests, and challenges
  • Daily messages, settings, theme preferences, reminder settings, and widget data

Data Collection and Sharing

Habit Seedling does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data with the developer or third parties.

The app does not currently use accounts, cloud sync, advertising, analytics, or developer-operated servers.

Notifications and Widgets

Habit Seedling may send local reminder notifications if you enable them. These reminders are generated on your device. Home screen widgets also use app data stored locally on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete habits and related app data inside Habit Seedling. You can also delete the app and its local data by uninstalling it from your device.

Children's Privacy

Habit Seedling is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if Habit Seedling changes how it handles data. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support email listed on Habit Seedling's Google Play Store page.
